This is a generic checklist of backpacking gear for a basic fair-weather trip. Items marked with an asterisk are optional, but good to have. Click on each link to see our in depth-guide to that piece of gear.

### Backpacking Gear

- Backpack with pack liner or cover
- Shelter
- Sleeping bag/quilt
- Sleeping pad
- Trekking poles \*

### Food and Water

- Stove and fuel
- Cooking pot
- Eating utensil
- Camp towel \*
- Water bottles
- [Water treatment](/gear/kitchen/water-treatment)
- Food
- Lighter
- Food bag
- Garbage bag

### Clothing and Footwear

- Underwear
- Hiking shirt
- Hiking shorts/pants
- Fleece or Puffy jacket
- Hiking shoes
- Hiking socks

### Electronics

- Smartphone
- External battery
- Charging cables
- Headlamp
- Earbuds \*

### Personal Care

- First aid kit
- Toothbrush and toothpaste
- Toilet paper
- Trowel
- Menstrual products
- Prescriptions
- Sunscreen \*
- Bug spray \*

### Tools and Miscellania

- Knife or mini scissors
- Repair tape
- Hiker wallet
- Car key
